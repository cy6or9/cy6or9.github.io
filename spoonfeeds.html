Simplefite

Simplefite is a bash scripted gui, simplifying wifite. Each attack is set to automatically take place, simply choose a selection and walk away once you see the scan taking place. Anything labeled "loop" is set to scan infinitely and wont stop until you CTRL+C the xterm window. Then you can select the "cracked" option to list any networks you achieved.

UUO

UUO (Unidentified User Options) is another bash script, but this one is written to focus on keeping you anonymous. It combines anonsurf, torbrowser, macchanger to help keep you hidden. It's equipped with a list of onion sites and nmap to check your own ip for lose ends. This is a work in progress, so forgive the randomness for now. I'd also like to move away from using anonsurf. Stay tuned for this ones development.

Torrenting anonymously

DO NOT torrent copyright material while testing. Find something free and preferably big. The bigger, the more time you'll have to analyze the peer and tracker list while downloading. Use a VPN that supports torrents. Know your original ip and watch the peer list for it. If your actual IP is shown your VPN is crap.
