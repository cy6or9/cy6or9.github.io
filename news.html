To prevent spreading the Coronavirus (COVID-19), you should wash your hands, use a hand sanitizer that is more than 60% alcohol, avoid touching your face (specifically your eyes, nose, and mouth), and keep distance between you and anyone who seems ill like. If you're already sick you should be checked into a hospital, cover your coughs while immediately washing your hands, throw used tissues in the trash, wear a mask around others, disinfect and clean any thing close-by or that you come into contact with.
